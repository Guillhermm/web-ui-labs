How to Play

• Swipe or use the arrow keys to move all tiles.

• When tiles collide, 2 or 3 consecutive tiles may merge. The merge of 3 tiles has precedence, as long as it is valid.

• The sum of the merged tiles must be a prime number and not greater than the "Next" value.

• If no valid merge is possible, the tiles simply move without merging.

• Each valid merge increases your score by the value of the new prime.

• The goal is to reach the highest prime possible.

Medals

• Reach last prime below 50 to earn a bronze medal.

• Reach last prime below 75 to earn a silver medal.

• Reach last prime below 100 to earn a gold medal.

About

Primes was developed by Guilherme Zeni. It is a variation of the original 2048 game by Gabriele Cirulli, using prime numbers and a different merge logic.
