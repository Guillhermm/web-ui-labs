How to Play

• Swipe or use arrow keys to move tiles.

• Merge 2 or 3 adjacent primes.

• The sum must be a prime ≤ the “Next” value.

• Try to reach the highest prime possible.

• Highest prime before 50 wins bronze.

• Highest prime before 75 wins silver.

• Highest prime before 100 wins gold.
